Update/Reset

Your Brew Bubbles can be updated over the internet to the latest version of the Brew Bubbles firmware by clicking the "Update Firmware" button below. Additionally, if you plan on relocating your Brew Bubbles to a new network, you can reset the WiFi connection settings by clicking "Reset WiFi".
